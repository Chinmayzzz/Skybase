We Deal Exclusively In

Bitumen

Bitumen is a viscous, black, and sticky substance derived from crude oil, primarily used in road construction and waterproofing applications. Known for its durability and adhesive properties, it serves as a key binder in asphalt mixes for paving and infrastructure projects.

Carbon Black FeedStock (CBFS)

Carbon black feedstock is a heavy aromatic oil used as a raw material in the production of carbon black, a fine black powder primarily used as a reinforcing agent in tires and as a pigment. It is derived from coal tar or petroleum residues and undergoes thermal decomposition to produce carbon black.

Coal

Coal is a combustible black or brownish-black sedimentary rock formed from ancient plant matter, widely used as a fuel source for electricity generation and industrial processes. Categorized into types like anthracite,bituminous,sub-bituminous,and lignite based on carbon content and energy output.
